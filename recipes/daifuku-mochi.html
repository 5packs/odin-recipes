Dafuku Mochi

Description

A popular Japanese sweet, Daifuku is a small, round mochi filled with sweet red bean paste. This easy recipe shows how you can make this delicious snack at home with a quick steam on the stovetop or in the microwave.

Ingredients

  • Sweet glutinous rice flour
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Anko paste>
  • Potato Starch

Steps

  1. Make the mochi mixture
  2. Spread mochi into thin layer
  3. Refrigarate for 15 minutes
  4. Cut the chilled mochi
  5. Stuff mochi with the anko filling
